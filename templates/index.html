Instructions:

  • Age: Age of the patient (in years)
  • Sex: Sex of the patient (1 = male, 0 = female)
  • Chest Pain Type (cp): Chest pain type (1-4)
  • Resting Blood Pressure (trestbps): Resting blood pressure (in mm Hg on admission to the hospital)
  • Cholesterol (chol): Serum cholesterol (mg/dl)
  • Fasting Blood Sugar (fbs): Fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl (1 = true, 0 = false)
  • Resting Electrocardiographic Results (restecg): Resting electrocardiographic results (0-2)
  • Maximum Heart Rate (thalach): Maximum heart rate achieved
  • Exercise-induced Angina (exang): Exercise-induced angina (1 = yes, 0 = no)
  • Oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest (min=0, max=6)
  • Slope: Slope of the peak exercise ST segment (1 = upsloping, 2 = flat, 3 = downsloping)
  • CA (ca): Number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy
  • Thalassemia (thal): Thalassemia (3 = normal, 6 = fixed defect, 7 = reversible defect)
